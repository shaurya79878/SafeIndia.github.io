  • One-Click Call for Help: Connect to police, ambulance, fire, and disaster response with a single tap.
  • Live Location Sharing: Automatically sends GPS location to emergency responders.

About Us

Shaurya Sahayak is a national help platform dedicated to providing essential emergency services information and resources across India. Our mission is to make India safer by offering a comprehensive and easily accessible platform for citizens in need. We leverage futuristic technologies like AI and 3D visualization to enhance emergency response and preparedness.
